Project Overview

Our website, Majorizer, will connect Students and Advisors in a way that allows them to build, modify, and approve a schedule to complete a four year undergraduate degree at Clarkson University. Majorizor will help users decide what courses they or their students should take, and will provide valuable insight for planning their path.
